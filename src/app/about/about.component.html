Purpose

Vote Plan provides a place to access information about elections and candidates.

The website aims to help you prepare your vote before voting day.

Privacy

Vote Plan stores information about your voting preferences in your browser only.

No details of the candidates you select are stored outside of your local browser or sent to remote servers, by the website creator.

Please note that browsers may have built-in or add-on functionality that can access webpages and send information to remote locations. Vote Plan cannot control this.

Vote Plan is hosted on Github Pages, which will have a record of the urls you access.

This website may use local browser storage mechanisms, including but not limited to cookies and local storage.

Vote Plan does not use visitor analytics or similar tracking tools that gather information about website visitors.
